**Text on Off-White Background:**
- Off-Black (#1F1F1F): 17.2:1 âœ… AAA
- Dark Gray (#404040): 9.6:1 âœ… AAA
- Purple (#7D53FA): 3.4:1 âœ… AA Large
- Green (#36EF79): 1.5:1 âŒ Use for decorative only
- Orange (#FD982E): 1.7:1 âŒ Use for decorative only

**Use This Rule:**
- Body text: Off-Black or Dark Gray only
- Headings: Off-Black, Dark Gray, or Purple
- Decorative: Any color with thick black borders
- Interactive: Ensure 3:1 contrast minimum
